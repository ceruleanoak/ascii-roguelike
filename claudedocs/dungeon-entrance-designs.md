# Dungeon Entrance Designs — Zone-Specific Exteriors

Room = 30×30 cells. One char per cell. Edit directly.
Player enters from south (row ~27, col 15).

Face anatomy: objects OUTSIDE the wall frame form the creature face.
  Row 6  = brow (objects flanking the forehead wall)
  Row 8  = eyes (objects flanking the temple walls)
  Row 10 = mouth/jaw (the solid wall row with ∩ door)
  Row 11 = chin flanks

**Cell key:**
```
#  border wall
.  empty floor
≡  solid wall (collision = true)
∩  door (interactable, not solid)
&  Tree       %  Bush       Y  Stump
0  Rock       Q  Boulder    !  Fire
8  Bones      i  Ice        *  Crystal
B  Metal Box  n  Mushroom
```

Wall colors and door colors listed per zone.
No enemy spawns in any dungeon room (same as maze).

## Implementation Notes

- Face structure (all zones except Yellow): solid ≡ at cols 12–18, rows 6,10; cols 12,18 rows 7–9. Door ∩ at row 10, col 15.
- Yellow: solid ≡ at cols 11–19, rows 6,10; cols 11,19 rows 7–9. Door ∩ at row 10, col 15.
- "Eye" objects (n/!/*/B/0) at cols 10,20 row 8 — non-solid, placed OUTSIDE the wall frame.
- "Brow" objects (Y/Q/i*/B/8) at cols 9,21 row 6 — non-solid, flanking the forehead wall.
- No generateBackgroundObjects call — full clearing zone covers entire room.
- No enemy spawns. exitsLocked = false.
- dungeon.doorPosition = { col: 15, row: 10 } for all zones.
